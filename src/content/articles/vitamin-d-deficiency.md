## What is Vitamin D Deficiency?

Vitamin D deficiency is common worldwide and can affect bone health, immunity and mood.

### Symptoms

- Fatigue
- Bone pain
- Muscle weakness

### Evidence-Based Treatment

Supplementation under medical supervision.
